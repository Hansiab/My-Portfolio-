About Me

Passionate UI/UX Designer & Engineer dedicated to creating meaningful digital experiences through innovative design and user-centered solutions.

My Story

As a Bachelor of Design student at the University of Moratuwa, specializing in Media and Communication Design, academic knowledge complements practical expertise. Google certifications in UX design and marketing further solidify a commitment to delivering impactful, user-centered designs.

To further demonstrate my creativity and commitment to the field, I actively work as a freelance graphic designer and digital, pencil portrait artist in addition to my studies. I also started an online jewelry business, turning my hobby into a profitable side gig.

UI/UX design is where I spend most of my time, and I'm always seeking to learn new things and enhance my skills. I am ready to add my knowledge and originality to interesting UI/UX design projects.
